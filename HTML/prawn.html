PRAWN BIRYANI

INGREDIENTS

  • Prawns - 500 g
  • Basmati Rice - 500 g
  • Curd - 1/4 cup
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric - 1/2 tsp
  • Lemon juice - 1/2 tbsp
  • Onion - 2 big (sliced)
  • Tomato - 1 medium
  • Green chilli - 3
  • Mint leaves - 1 cup
  • Coriander leaves - 1 cup
  • Oil - 3 tbsp
  • Ghee - 2 tbsp
  • Bay leaf - 1
  • Cinnamon - 1 piece
  • Cloves - 3
  • Cardamom - 3
  • Star anise - 1
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1.5 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Salt as needed

PROCESS

  1. Step 1: Prawns-ah clean panni curd, turmeric, lemon, ginger-garlic paste podi-vechu 10 mins marinate pannu.
  2. Step 2: Pan la oil + ghee heat panni whole spices splutter panna vidu.
  3. Step 3: Onion add panni golden aagum vara fry pannu.
  4. Step 4: Tomato + green chilli add panni soft aagum vara saute pannu.
  5. Step 5: Mint + coriander leaves add panni 1 minute saute pannu.
  6. Step 6: Red chilli, coriander, garam masala podi add panni mix pannu.
  7. Step 7: Marinated prawns add panni 4-5 mins only cook pannu (prawns fast-a cook aagum).
  8. Step 8: Rice add panni salt adjust panni mix pannu.
  9. Step 9: Water add panni dum vechu biryani cook panna vidu.
  10. Step 10: 10-12 mins low flame la super prawn biryani ready..!üòç
